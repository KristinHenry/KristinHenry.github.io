Welcome to my Flower Maker

A simple creative tool, that generates abstract flowers.

--n: 20; --colorMin: 190; --colorMax: 280; --color: @rand(colorMin,colorMax); @grid: 20 x 1 / 40em; @place-cell: center; @size: calc(100% / var(--n) * @index); z-index: calc(var(--n) - @index); background: hsla(@r360, 70%, 70%, @r.9); clip-path: @shape( points: 600; r: cos(@pick(2,3,4,6,8,10,12,13,14,15)t) );

Click flower to generate a new pattern.
